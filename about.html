Our Mission

Here at J&P Associates Ltd., we aim to help those who need it by providing financial services for those who are dependent on government benefits, such as Social Security and SSI. Our company's number one drive is our clients and we work everyday to ensure they are receiving the best possible service.

J&P Associates Ltd At A Glance

A Bit Of Background

Since being established in 1999, J&P Associates has been a federally tax-exempt 501C3 state non-profit representative payee organization who provides financial pay assistance for those who are dependent on the government for their income. We do charge a fee for each client that is regulated by the Social Security Administration. Currently, our fee is $43 per month.
